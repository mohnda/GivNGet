What is GivNGet?

You can find someone to do ANYTHING on GivNGet.co.nz. Picking up drycleaning, mowing your lawn, and cooking your dinner are examples of tasks you can post.

How do I Give Help?

People post their tasks on GivNGet.co.nz. All you have to do is bid on a task. If an employer accepts your bid, you do the task and then get paid!

Learn about getting stuff done

How can I Get Help?

Post a task and people will bid on your task. All you have to do is choose your most preferred candidate.They will get the task done and you pay them!
